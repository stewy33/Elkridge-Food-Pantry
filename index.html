The Elkridge Food Pantry is a non-profit and totally volunteer run organization. We are located at the Good Samaritan Outreach Center in Elkridge. The pantry is here to serve all residents of Elkridge in both temporary and long-term need. We are open the second and fourth Friday of each month.
